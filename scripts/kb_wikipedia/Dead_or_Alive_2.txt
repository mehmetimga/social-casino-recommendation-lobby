Title: Dead or Alive 2
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

Dead or Alive 2 is NetEnt's legendary sequel to one of the most iconic high-volatility slots ever created. Released in 2019, it takes the Wild West theme to extreme levels with three different free spins modes and massive win potential up to 111,111x bet.

Game Mechanics:
5 reels, 3 rows, 9 paylines. The simple structure belies incredible depth through its innovative free spins options. Wild substitutions and sticky wilds drive the game's notorious big win potential.

RTP and Volatility:
96.8% RTP with extreme high volatility. This slot can go hundreds of spins without a feature, then deliver life-changing wins. Not for the faint of heart or small bankrolls.

Theme and Design:
Gritty Wild West atmosphere with wanted posters, sheriff badges, cowboy boots, and whiskey bottles. The sequel enhances the original's aesthetic with sharper graphics while maintaining the dusty frontier feel. Atmospheric soundtrack builds tension.

Symbol Values:
- Five Wilds: 2,500 coins (top base game win)
- Five Scatters: 2,500 coins + bonus trigger
- Five High Noon symbols: 500 coins
- Cowboys, Outlaws: Medium value
- Card symbols (10-A): Low value

Wild Symbols:
Standard wilds substitute for all except scatters. In free spins, wilds become sticky and can carry multipliers depending on the mode chosen.

The Three Free Spins Modes:

1. Train Heist Free Spins:
- 12 free spins, medium volatility choice
- Multiplier wilds increase by 1x each spin
- Wilds can reach high multipliers over time
- Best for: Players wanting balanced risk

2. Old Saloon Free Spins:
- 12 free spins, lower volatility choice
- Sticky wilds remain for duration
- Multiplier wilds with random 2x or 3x
- Best for: More frequent but smaller wins

3. High Noon Saloon Free Spins:
- 12 free spins, EXTREME volatility
- All wilds are sticky for entire feature
- Wilds start at 2x and increase by 1x each spin they remain
- Filling all positions with wilds pays maximum win
- Best for: Risk-tolerant players seeking maximum potential

Each mode can retrigger by landing 2+ scatters, adding +5 spins.

Maximum Win:
111,111x your bet in High Noon mode. Achieved by filling all 15 positions with sticky multiplier wilds, each at maximum multiplier value. Extremely rare but documented wins exist.

Win Potential Breakdown:
- Train Heist: Up to 40,500x

- High Noon: Up to 111,111x

Strategy Considerations:

For Maximum Potential: Choose High Noon every time. The variance is extreme but it's the only path to the biggest wins.

For Balanced Play: Train Heist offers good potential with less brutal dry spells.

For Feature Experience: Old Saloon triggers wins more often within the feature itself.
