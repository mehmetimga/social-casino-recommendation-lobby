Title: Ancient Gems

Ancient Gems combines the allure of precious gemstones with archaeological adventure, taking players to forgotten temples where legendary jewels await discovery. This gem-matching slot features cluster pays and unique gem transformation mechanics.

Game Mechanics:
7x7 grid with cluster pays mechanics - win by connecting 5 or more matching gems horizontally or vertically. Winning clusters disappear and new gems cascade from above, allowing multiple wins from a single spin. The innovative gem transformation system can upgrade matching gems to higher-value stones.

RTP and Volatility:
Ancient Gems offers 96.4% RTP with medium-high volatility. The cluster pay system provides exciting cascade sequences, while the gem transformation feature adds layers of strategic anticipation.

Gemstone Symbols:
Six tiers of precious gems grace the reels: Amethyst (lowest), Topaz, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, and Diamond (highest). Each gem tier has distinctive visual effects when part of winning clusters. The Ancient Crown serves as the wild symbol.

Gem Transformation Feature:
When you achieve 3 consecutive cascade wins with the same gem type, that gem transforms into the next tier up. For example, if you win with Amethyst three times in one spin sequence, all Amethysts transform into Topaz for the remainder of that spin.

Temple Exploration Free Spins:
Landing a special Temple Key symbol triggers free spins. Players choose their exploration path:
- Safe Path: 15 spins with 2x multiplier
- Risky Path: 10 spins with escalating 1x-5x multiplier
- Treasure Path: 8 spins with starting 3x multiplier and gem transformation boost

Ancient Artifact Collection:
Collect artifact pieces during gameplay. Complete artifacts unlock persistent upgrades: increased gem transformation frequency, starting multiplier boosts, and extra free spins.

Earthquake Feature:
Randomly during play, an earthquake shakes the temple, shuffling all gems on the grid. This can break losing positions into winning clusters or enhance existing matches.

Visual and Audio:
Detailed temple interior with flickering torchlight and ancient stone carvings. Gems sparkle with realistic light refraction. The soundtrack features mysterious percussion and atmospheric ambient sounds.
