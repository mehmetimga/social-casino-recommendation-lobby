Fruit Party by Pragmatic Play is a vibrant cluster pays slot that delivers high volatility thrills with a cheerful fruit theme. Released in 2020, it quickly became one of Pragmatic's most popular titles thanks to its simple mechanics and impressive 5,000x maximum win potential.

Game Mechanics:
7x7 grid with cluster pays. Win by connecting 5+ matching fruit symbols horizontally or vertically. Cascading wins (tumble feature) remove winning clusters and drop new symbols for potential chain reactions.

RTP and Volatility:
96.47% RTP with high volatility. The combination of large grid, multipliers, and feature potential creates significant variance.

Random Multiplier Feature:
The key mechanic:
- Some symbols randomly become multipliers (2x or more)
- Multiplier symbols that form winning clusters apply multiplier to win

- Creates massive win potential

- Cluster with 2x, 4x, 2x: Cluster win × 16

Free Spins Feature:

Tumble Feature:
Every win triggers a tumble:
1. Winning symbols disappear
2. Remaining fruits fall
3. New fruits fill empty spaces
4. Check for new wins
5. Repeat until no wins
