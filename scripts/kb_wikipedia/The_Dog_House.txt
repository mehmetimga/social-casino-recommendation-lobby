Title: The Dog House
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

The Dog House by Pragmatic Play is one of the most popular slot series ever created. The original, released in 2019, spawned numerous sequels thanks to its winning combination of cute theme, sticky wilds with multipliers, and high volatility gameplay.

Game Mechanics:
5 reels, 3 rows, 20 paylines. Simple structure enhanced by sticky wilds with multipliers during free spins - the mechanic that made this slot famous.

RTP and Volatility:
96.51% RTP with high volatility. The game can swing dramatically between sessions, with free spins capable of producing huge multiplied wins.

Theme and Design:
Adorable cartoon dogs in their backyard doghouses. Colorful, cheerful graphics with lovable pups wearing various accessories. Despite the cute theme, the mechanics deliver serious winning potential.

The Dog House Family:
This slot spawned a hugely successful series:
- The Dog House (Original)

- And more variants

- Rottweiler: Highest value
- Pug: High value

- Card symbols: Low value

Wild Symbol:
The doghouse is wild, substituting for all except scatters. In free spins, wilds become sticky and carry multipliers - the key to big wins.

Scatter Symbol:
The paw print scatter triggers free spins:
- 3 Scatters: 9 free spins
- 4 Scatters: 12 free spins
- 5 Scatters: 15 free spins

Free Spins Feature:
Where the magic happens:
- Wilds that land become sticky for entire feature
- Each wild carries a random 2x or 3x multiplier
- Multiple wild multipliers MULTIPLY together
- Landing wilds extends sticky positions
- Can retrigger with 3+ scatters (+9 spins)

Multiplier Math:
This is what creates huge wins:
- Two 3x wilds = 9x total multiplier
- Three 3x wilds = 27x total multiplier
- Four 3x wilds = 81x total multiplier
- Five 3x wilds = 243x total multiplier

Combined with line wins, these multipliers can create massive payouts.

Maximum Win:
Up to 6,750x bet in the original version. Later variants like Megaways offer even higher potential (up to 12,305x).

Strategy Considerations:
1. Base game is standard - real value in features
2. Pray for 3x multipliers on sticky wilds
3. More wilds = exponentially bigger potential
4. Retriggers extend the opportunity for wild accumulation
5. Patience required between features
