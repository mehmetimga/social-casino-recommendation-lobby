Title: Rise of Olympus
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

Rise of Olympus by Play'n GO brings Greek mythology to a 5x5 grid with spectacular god powers and massive win potential. Released in 2018, this cluster-style slot features Zeus, Poseidon, and Hades helping players achieve divine victories.

Game Mechanics:
5x5 grid with symbol drops (cascading wins). Win by connecting 3+ matching symbols horizontally or vertically. After wins, symbols disappear and new ones fall. No traditional paylines - it's all about matching clusters.

RTP and Volatility:
96.5% RTP with high volatility. The combination of god powers, multipliers, and free spins creates significant variance.

Theme and Design:
Mount Olympus setting with stunning Greek temple visuals. Three gods stand beside the reels, activating when their symbols win. Lightning, waves, and hellfire accompany the god powers. Dramatic orchestral soundtrack elevates the experience.

The Three Gods:

Zeus (Yellow):
- Storm God
- Hand of God Power: Destroys 2 random symbol types

Poseidon (Blue):
- Sea God
- Hand of God Power: 1-2 wild symbols added

Hades (Red):
- Underworld God
- Hand of God Power: Transforms one symbol type to another

God Powers Activation:
When you win with a god's symbols, their power may randomly activate after the cascade completes. This creates additional winning opportunities.

Wrath of Olympus Feature:
Triggered when no winning combinations land:
- Random god power activates
- Gives second chance at wins
- Prevents complete dead spins

Meter System:
Right side of screen shows a meter that fills with winning symbols:
- Fill the meter partially: Unlock individual god features
- Clear ALL symbols from grid: Trigger Free Spins

This creates exciting tension as the grid approaches empty.

Free Spins:
Triggered by clearing all 25 positions (very difficult):
- Choose your god for free spins
- Each god offers different feature

Zeus Free Spins: Multiplier increases with each cascade. Most volatile.
Poseidon Free Spins: Wild symbols guaranteed during feature.
Hades Free Spins: Symbol transformation powers enhanced.

All modes start with 4 free spins, +4 for each additional grid clear.

Multiplier System (Zeus Spins):
- Starts at 1x
- Increases with each winning cascade
- Can reach very high multipliers
- Persists through feature

Maximum Win:
Up to 5,000x bet through multiplied grid clears in free spins.

Strategy Considerations:
1. God powers are random - can't control them
2. Grid clears are rare but trigger free spins
3. Zeus free spins offer highest potential
4. Wrath feature saves many dead spins
5. Patience for the rare big wins

Bankroll Guidelines:
- Minimum: 150x bet
- Comfortable: 300x bet
- Recommended: 500x bet
