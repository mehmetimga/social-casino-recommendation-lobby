Book of Dead by Play'n GO is the legendary Egyptian adventure slot that took the industry by storm. Following the fearless explorer Rich Wilde through ancient tombs, this high-volatility masterpiece offers thrilling gameplay and massive win potential up to 5,000x.

Game Structure:
5 reels, 3 rows, and 10 adjustable paylines set against the backdrop of an Egyptian temple. The RTP is 96.21%, and the high volatility means wins can be infrequent but potentially enormous when they hit.

Symbol Paytable:
- Rich Wilde (Explorer): Highest paying regular symbol, 5000x for five
- Gods (Anubis, Ra, Osiris): Premium symbols paying 2000x, 750x, 750x
- Card Symbols (10-A): Lower paying but frequent wins
- Book of Dead: Both wild and scatter symbol

The Book of Dead Feature:
Landing 3, 4, or 5 Book symbols anywhere triggers 10 free spins with a special expanding symbol. Before spins begin, one symbol is randomly chosen to become the special expanding symbol. During free spins, when this symbol appears, it expands to cover entire reels before pays are evaluated, potentially creating massive line wins.

The expanding symbol pays on any positions (doesn't need to be adjacent), and free spins can be retriggered with 3+ Books, awarding 10 additional spins.

Winning Strategy:
The key to Book of Dead is the expanding symbol choice during free spins. Landing Rich Wilde as the expanding symbol offers the highest potential (up to 250,000 coins on a single spin). However, even medium symbols can deliver spectacular wins when they fill the screen.

Many experienced players adjust their bet just before triggering the bonus, as the game determines the special symbol based on an algorithm that may favor certain betting patterns. While not guaranteed, bet variation is a common strategy.

Max Win Potential:
The theoretical maximum win is 5,000x your bet, achievable when Rich Wilde (or another high symbol) expands across all 5 reels during the free spins bonus. Real players have won hundreds of thousands from this feature.
