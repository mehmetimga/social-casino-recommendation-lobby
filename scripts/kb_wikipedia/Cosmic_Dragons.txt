Cosmic Dragons takes the beloved dragon theme into the far reaches of space, featuring magnificent space-dwelling dragons that soar through nebulae and guard celestial treasures. This visually spectacular slot combines fantasy and sci-fi elements for a truly unique experience.

Game Mechanics:
6 reels, 4 rows with 4,096 ways to win. The game features a Dragon Sync mechanic where matching dragons on different reels can synchronize and all transform into the same symbol. Cascading wins with increasing multipliers keep the action flowing.

RTP and Volatility:
Cosmic Dragons offers 96.5% RTP with high volatility. The combination of ways-to-win, dragon sync, and cascade multipliers creates potential for massive payouts during hot streaks.

Cosmic Dragon Symbols:
Five elemental space dragons inhabit the reels:
- Nova Dragon (fire/red) - Highest value
- Nebula Dragon (purple/cosmic)
- Stellar Dragon (gold/light)
- Void Dragon (black/shadow)
- Aurora Dragon (green/energy)
The Cosmic Egg serves as both wild and scatter.

Dragon Sync Feature:
When 2 or more different dragons land on separate reels, there's a chance they'll synchronize. All synced dragons transform into the highest-value dragon among them. Full sync (all 6 reels) is rare but triggers the Dragon Constellation bonus.

Cosmic Cascade:
Like other cascade games, winning symbols vanish and new ones fall in. Each cascade increases the multiplier: 1x → 2x → 3x → 5x → 10x → 15x (max). Multipliers persist through the entire cascade sequence.

Celestial Free Spins:
Landing 4, 5, or 6 Cosmic Egg symbols awards 10, 15, or 25 free spins. During free spins:
- Dragon Sync triggers more frequently
- Cascade multiplier starts at 2x and has no cap
- Each additional Cosmic Egg during free spins adds +3 spins

Dragon Constellation Bonus:
Achieving full Dragon Sync (all 6 reels) triggers this special bonus. Players navigate a star map, choosing paths through constellations. Each choice reveals prizes: multipliers, coin values, or the Cosmic Jackpot.

Nova Burst:
Random feature that can trigger after any non-winning spin. The Nova Dragon appears and breathes cosmic fire across the reels, transforming multiple positions into matching dragon symbols, guaranteeing a win.
