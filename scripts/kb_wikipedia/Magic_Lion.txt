Title: Magic Lion

Magic Lion draws inspiration from the legendary Chinese guardian lions, mythical creatures believed to possess powerful protective magic. This feature-rich slot combines traditional Asian symbolism with magical transformations and roaring bonus features.

Game Mechanics:
5 reels, 4 rows with 1,024 ways to win provide massive winning potential on every spin. The Ways-to-Win system means matching symbols on adjacent reels from left to right pay regardless of row position. The Magic Lion can appear as a colossal 2x2 or 3x3 symbol.

RTP and Volatility:
Magic Lion features 96.6% RTP with high volatility. The 1,024 ways system combined with colossal symbols creates potential for screen-filling wins with substantial payouts.

Symbol Design:
Traditional Chinese lucky symbols fill the reels including golden coins, red lanterns, firecrackers, and jade pendants. The Magic Lion serves as both wild and the key to the main bonus features. The Fortune Gong is the scatter symbol.

Colossal Lion Feature:
The Magic Lion can appear as a giant 2x2 or 3x3 symbol, covering multiple positions. When colossal lions land, they count as multiple symbols for the ways calculation, dramatically increasing win potential. A 3x3 lion effectively counts as 9 wild symbols.

Lion's Roar Respin:
When a Magic Lion lands covering at least 4 positions, it triggers Lion's Roar Respin. The lion roars and freezes in place while all other reels respin once, giving extra chances for winning combinations.

Fortune Gong Free Spins:
Landing 3, 4, or 5 Fortune Gong scatters awards 8, 12, or 20 free spins. During free spins, colossal lions appear more frequently, and each colossal lion that lands adds +1 to your free spin count.

Golden Guardian Jackpot:
A four-tier jackpot system (Mini, Minor, Major, Grand) can trigger when special jackpot coins land. Collect 3 matching jackpot coins in a single spin to win the corresponding jackpot prize.

Visual and Audio:
Ornate Chinese temple setting with golden lion statues that animate during big wins. Red and gold color scheme with particle effects for magical transformations. Powerful drum beats and traditional Chinese instruments create an epic atmosphere.

Maximum Win:
Magic Lion offers maximum win potential of 6,888x your bet (888 being a lucky number in Chinese culture), achievable through multiple colossal lions during free spins with jackpot combinations.
