Sun of Egypt by Booongo is a visually stunning Egyptian-themed slot that captures the glory of ancient pharaohs and the blazing desert sun. This medium-volatility game offers a perfect balance of excitement and accessibility for all players.

Game Structure:
5 reels, 3 rows, and 25 paylines featuring classic Egyptian iconography. The golden sunburst wild symbol expands across entire reels when it lands, creating massive winning potential.

Symbol Paytable:
- Pharaoh: Highest paying symbol, 40x for five
- Queen Cleopatra: Premium symbol, 20x for five
- Scarab Beetle: Sacred symbol, 15x for five
- Eye of Horus: Protection symbol, 10x for five
- Ankh: Life symbol, 8x for five
- Card symbols (A-10): Lower paying symbols

Sun Wild Feature:
The golden sun wild is the key to big wins. When it lands on reels 2, 3, or 4, it expands to cover the entire reel and triggers a free re-spin. Multiple expanding wilds can create full-screen wins.

Free Spins Bonus:
Landing 3 or more pyramid scatters triggers 10 free spins. During free spins, the sun wild appears more frequently and all wins are multiplied by 3x.

Gamble Feature:
After any win, players can gamble their winnings by guessing the color of the next card. Correct guesses double the prize.

Maximum Win:
Sun of Egypt offers a maximum win of 1,250x your bet, achievable through multiple expanding wilds during free spins.
