Title: Razor Shark
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

Razor Shark by Push Gaming is a beloved high-volatility slot that combines an underwater theme with innovative mystery stacks and a unique nudge mechanic. Released in 2019, it quickly became a fan favorite for its exciting gameplay and massive win potential.

Game Mechanics:
5 reels, 4 rows, 20 paylines. The Mystery Stacks feature drives most of the action, with symbols that reveal identical values. The Nudge and Reveal mechanic adds suspense as mystery stacks move down the reels across multiple spins.

RTP and Volatility:
96.70% RTP with high volatility. Razor Shark can be streaky, with extended quiet periods followed by explosive wins from the free spins feature.

Theme and Design:
Vibrant underwater setting with a menacing great white shark as the star. Crystal-clear blue water, scattered seaweed, and playful fish create an immersive oceanic atmosphere. The shark animation during big wins is memorable and exciting.

Symbol Values:
- Shark (Wild): Substitutes for all except Scatter
- Diver: Highest paying regular symbol
- Camera: High value
- Oxygen Tank: Medium value
- Diving Mask, Fins: Medium value
- Card symbols (A-J): Low value

Mystery Stacks:
The core mechanic. Mystery Stack symbols appear covered in seaweed on reels 2, 3, and 4. They can:
- Reveal matching symbols for big wins
- Reveal Golden Shark symbols (multipliers or instant prizes)
- Trigger the Nudge and Reveal feature

Nudge and Reveal:
When Mystery Stacks don't fully reveal on one spin:
- Remaining mystery positions nudge down
- New positions added at top
- Continues until no mystery symbols remain or feature triggers
- Creates building tension and multi-spin excitement

Golden Shark Symbols:
Revealed from Mystery Stacks:
- Instant coin wins (various sizes)
- Multipliers applied to wins
- Can reveal Scatter symbols
- Razorback symbol awards entire stack value

Razor Reveal Feature:
Triggered when Mystery Stack reveals all Golden Sharks:
- Whole stack awards combined value
- Can result in massive single-spin wins
- The "Razorback" moment players hope for

Free Spins:
Triggered by 3+ Scatter symbols:
- 3 Scatters: 8 free spins
- 4 Scatters: 12 free spins
- 5 Scatters: 16 free spins

During Free Spins:
- Guaranteed Mystery Stacks every spin
- Higher chance of Golden Sharks
- Multiplier accumulates from Golden Shark reveals
- Can retrigger with 3+ Scatters

Maximum Win:
Up to 50,000x bet, achieved through accumulated multipliers in free spins combined with Razor Reveal moments.

Strategy Tips:
1. This slot requires patience - base game can be slow
2. Mystery Stacks in base game often lead nowhere
3. Free spins is where the real potential lies
4. Adequate bankroll essential for the variance
5. Watch for consecutive Mystery Stack lands

Bankroll Recommendations:
- Minimum: 200x bet
- Comfortable: 400-500x bet
- Ideal for extended play: 800x+ bet

Why Players Love It:

Feature Potential: Free spins with the multiplier mechanic can produce incredible wins.
