Key Features:

Stacked Wolf Symbols:
The wolf (highest paying symbol) can appear stacked on all reels. Landing full stacks of wolves creates spectacular line hits across multiple paylines, as

 one full reel of wolves can contribute to numerous winning combinations simultaneously.

Wild Symbol:
The sunset wild substitutes for all symbols except scatters and money symbols. Wilds only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4, but when they land, they significantly boost winning potential.

Free Spins Feature:
Landing 3 or more scatter symbols (desert canyon scene) triggers 5 free spins. While 5 spins seems short, the feature has incredible potential due to the special mechanics:

During free spins, reels 2, 3, and 4 become merged into one giant symbol area. This creates "mega symbols" that act as massive 3x3 blocks. When a mega symbol lands, it covers the entire middle section, creating guaranteed wins across multiple paylines.

Example: A mega eagle symbol covers all 9 positions in the middle 3 reels, creating numerous winning combinations with matching symbols on reels 1 and 5.

Money Respin Feature:
Landing 6 or more Money symbols (full moons with cash values) triggers the Money Respin. Money symbols remain sticky, and you get 3 respins. Each new Money symbol that lands resets the respin counter to 3.

During respins:
- Money symbols show cash prizes
- Landing new Money symbols adds to accumulated total
- Filling all 15 positions triggers the Mega Jackpot
- Three jackpot levels are available: Mini, Major, and Mega

Progressive Jackpots:
- Mini Jackpot: Smallest progressive, hits frequently
- Major Jackpot: Mid-sized progressive
- Mega Jackpot: The big prize, awarded when all 15 positions fill with Money symbols

The jackpots are pooled across all casinos offering Wolf Gold, allowing them to grow to significant amounts.
