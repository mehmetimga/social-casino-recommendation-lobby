Title: Blazing Carnival
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

Blazing Carnival brings the excitement and color of a world-class carnival to the reels with a fiery twist. This vibrant slot game captures the magic of carnival nights with flaming performances, dazzling fireworks, and prize-filled attractions that keep players on the edge of their seats.

Game Mechanics:
5 reels, 4 rows with 40 paylines offer plenty of action. The game features a unique Blazing Respins mechanism where fire-framed symbols lock in place and respins continue until no new blazing symbols appear.

RTP and Volatility:
Blazing Carnival offers 96.3% RTP with medium volatility, providing a balanced gaming experience with regular wins and exciting bonus features. Perfect for extended play sessions without excessive bankroll swings.

Carnival Theme and Symbols:
The colorful reels showcase carnival performers including fire breathers, acrobats, ringmasters, and exotic dancers. Carnival treats like cotton candy and caramel apples serve as lower-paying symbols. The Fire Breather is the wild symbol, and the Big Top tent is the scatter.

Blazing Respins Feature:
When 6 or more fire-framed symbols land anywhere on the reels, the Blazing Respins feature triggers. All blazing symbols lock in place displaying coin values, and players receive 3 respins. Each new blazing symbol resets the respin counter. Landing 15 blazing symbols (full screen) awards the Grand Prize.

Prize Wheel Bonus:
Landing 3 or more Big Top scatter symbols triggers the Prize Wheel bonus. Players spin a carnival wheel featuring multipliers from 5x to 100x, extra free spins, or direct cash prizes. Multiple wheel spins may be awarded.

Fire Juggler Free Spins:
During free spins, the Fire Juggler may randomly appear and throw flaming torches onto the reels, converting regular symbols into fire-framed symbols for enhanced Blazing Respin potential.

Visual and Audio:
Stunning neon-lit carnival visuals with dynamic fire effects create an electrifying atmosphere. The soundtrack features upbeat carnival music with dramatic drum rolls during big wins and firework sound effects.

Maximum Win:
Blazing Carnival offers maximum win potential of 3,500x your bet, primarily achieved through full-screen Blazing Respins combined with accumulated coin values and multipliers.
