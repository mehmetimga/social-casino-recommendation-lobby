Silver and pearl-themed symbols replace the gold of previous entries. The Silver Empress is the highest-paying symbol, followed by the Moonlit Dragon. Silver lanterns, moon gates, and pearl ornaments fill the remaining positions. The Full Moon is both wild and scatter.

Day/Night Cycle:
The game alternates between day and night phases every 25 spins. During night phase:
- All silver symbols pay double
- Wilds expand to cover entire reels
- Free spin trigger chance doubles
- Moonlit Multipliers become active

Moonlit Multiplier Feature:
During night phase, random positions on the grid receive moonlight beams. Any winning symbol in a moonlit position receives a 2x, 3x, or 5x multiplier. Multiple moonlit multipliers in one win multiply together.

Lunar Eclipse Free Spins:
Landing 4, 5, or 6 Full Moon symbols triggers 10, 15, or 25 free spins during a permanent Lunar Eclipse. During the eclipse, all night phase bonuses are active with enhanced values - silver symbols pay triple instead of double.

Silver Dragon Bonus:
Randomly during night phase, the Silver Dragon may descend and breathe silver fire across the reels, transforming all low-paying symbols into silver high-paying symbols for that spin.
