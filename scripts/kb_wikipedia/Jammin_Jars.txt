Title: Jammin' Jars
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

Jammin' Jars by Push Gaming revolutionized slot design with its cluster pays mechanic and roaming wild multipliers. Released in 2018, this fruit-themed slot became an instant classic known for colorful visuals, funky music, and massive win potential.

Game Mechanics:
8x8 grid with cluster pays - no traditional paylines. Win by connecting 5+ matching symbols horizontally or vertically. Cascading wins remove winning clusters, dropping new symbols for potential chain reactions.

RTP and Volatility:
96.83% RTP with high volatility. The combination of cluster pays and multiplying wilds creates significant variance.

Theme and Design:
Funky fruit party with a DJ twist. Dancing fruits animate to an electronic soundtrack. Three colorful jam jars serve as wilds, bouncing around the grid between cascades. Vibrant, energetic atmosphere that matches the high-energy gameplay.

Cluster Pays System:
- 5 symbols: Small win
- 6-9 symbols: Medium win
- 10-14 symbols: Large win
- 15+ symbols: Massive win (rare but possible on 8x8 grid)

Larger clusters exponentially increase payouts. A 25+ symbol cluster can pay hundreds of times bet.

Jam Jar Wilds:
The stars of the show:
- Substitute for all fruits
- Start at 1x multiplier
- Increase by 1x for every winning cluster they help create
- Move to random adjacent position after each cascade
- Can combine (two jars contributing = multipliers multiply)

Multiplier Growth Example:
- First win with jar: 1x
- Second cascade: 2x
- Third cascade: 3x
- Fourth cascade: 4x
- And so on...

Multiplier combinations when multiple jars contribute:
- 3x and 4x jar = 12x multiplier
- 5x and 6x jar = 30x multiplier
- Three jars at 4x each = 64x multiplier

Free Spins:
Triggered by 3+ jam jars anywhere on grid in single spin:
- 3 jars: 6 free spins
- 4 jars: 8 free spins
- 5 jars: 10 free spins
- 6 jars: 12 free spins

During Free Spins:
- Giant symbols (up to 4x4) can appear
- Jars retain multipliers between spins
- More cascades = higher multipliers
- Can retrigger with 3+ jars

Giant Fruit Feature:
In free spins, oversized fruits (2x2, 3x3, or 4x4) can land. These count as multiple symbols toward clusters, making massive clusters more achievable.

Maximum Win:
Up to 20,000x bet through accumulated multipliers and giant fruit clusters in free spins.

Strategy Tips:
1. Hope for multiple jars to land near each other
2. Free spins is where potential unlocks
3. Watch for cascade chains building multipliers
4. Giant fruits in free spins are key to big wins
5. Jam jars meeting each other is the dream scenario

Bankroll Guidelines:
- Minimum: 150x bet
- Comfortable: 300x bet
- Recommended: 500x bet
