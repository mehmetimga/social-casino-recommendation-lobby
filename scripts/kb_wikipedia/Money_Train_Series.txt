Core Mechanic - Money Cart Bonus:
The signature feature across all games. When triggered:
1. Special reels with symbol positions appear
2. Symbols reveal coin values or special features
3. 3 respins that reset when new symbols land
4. Feature ends when no new symbols land
5. All revealed values paid at end

Special Symbols in Money Cart:

Persistent Payer: Pays its value every spin, not just once
Collector: Collects values of all visible symbols
Payer: Adds coins to all visible symbols
Sniper: Picks random symbol, removes and adds its value to itself
Necromancer: Brings back previously collected symbols
Bonus: Awards additional respins
