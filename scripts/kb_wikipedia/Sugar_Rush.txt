Sugar Rush by Pragmatic Play is a sweet-themed cluster pays slot that combines colorful candy visuals with explosive multiplier potential. Released in 2022, it quickly became one of Pragmatic's most popular titles, known for its 5,000x max win and satisfying cascading gameplay.

Game Mechanics:
7x7 grid with cluster pays. Win by connecting 5+ matching candy symbols horizontally or vertically. Cascading wins (tumble feature) remove winning clusters and drop new symbols. Multiplier spots on the grid amplify wins significantly.

RTP and Volatility:
96.50% RTP with high volatility. The combination of cluster pays and multiplier accumulation creates significant variance between sessions.

Multiplier Spots:
The key mechanic making Sugar Rush special:
- Random grid positions become multiplier spots
- When symbols win on these spots, multipliers activate
- Multipliers increase by 1x with each winning cluster on that spot
- Multipliers persist through cascades
- Multiple multipliers on same cluster MULTIPLY together

Multiplier Math Example:
- Cluster wins on spot with 4x multiplier: Win × 4
- Cluster spans two spots (3x and 5x): Win × 15
- Extended cascades can build spots to 10x, 20x, or more
