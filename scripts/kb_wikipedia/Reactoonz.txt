Title: Reactoonz
Source: Social Casino Knowledge Base
License: Internal

Reactoonz by Play'n GO is a beloved cluster pays slot featuring adorable alien creatures on a 7x7 grid. Released in 2017, its combination of cute characters, cascading wins, and multiple bonus features made it an instant classic that spawned a successful franchise.

Game Mechanics:
7x7 grid with cluster pays. Win by connecting 5+ matching symbols horizontally or vertically. Cascading wins remove clusters and drop new symbols. The Gargantoon wild and Quantum features add layers of excitement.

RTP and Volatility:
96.51% RTP with high volatility. The large grid and multiple features create significant variance between sessions.

Theme and Design:
Colorful alien creatures (Reactoonz) on a space/laboratory theme. Each alien has unique personality and animations. Playful, cartoon aesthetic appeals to wide audience. Fun sound effects accompany wins.

The Reactoonz Characters:
- One-Eyed Aliens (4 colors): Standard symbols
- Two-Eyed Aliens: Higher value
- Wild Symbol: Substitutes for all aliens
- Gargantoon: Giant 3x3 wild that transforms

Cluster Pays System:
- 5 symbols: Small payout
- 6-10 symbols: Medium payout
- 11-15 symbols: Good payout
- 16+ symbols: Excellent payout

On a 7x7 grid, massive clusters are possible.

Fluctuation (Charge Meter):
Key mechanic driving bonus features:
- Winning symbols charge the meter
- 4 electrons fill one charge level
- Each level unlocks a Quantum Feature
- 5 levels total to fill
- Meter resets when no more cascades

Quantum Features (in order):

Implosion: 3-6 wild symbols created, adjacent symbols destroyed
Incision: Wild cuts through center, creating more wilds
Demolition: All one-eyed aliens destroyed, others drop
Alteration: One random alien type transforms to another

When all four trigger in sequence, the fifth level unlocks...

Gargantoon Feature:
The ultimate reward:
- Giant 3x3 wild appears
- After cascade, divides into 2x2 wild
- Then splits into 9 individual wilds
- Creates massive win potential
- Most exciting moment in the game

Instability Feature:
If no winning combination:
- 4-8 wild symbols added to grid
- Gives second chance at cascades
- Prevents complete dead spins

Strategy Tips:
1. Watch the Fluctuation meter build
2. Large clusters charge meter faster
3. Gargantoon is the big win opportunity
4. Instability prevents total losses
5. Patience for cascade chains

Bankroll Recommendations:
- Minimum: 150x bet
- Comfortable: 300x bet
- Recommended: 500x bet

Maximum Win:
Up to 4,570x bet through Gargantoon combinations and extended cascade chains.
